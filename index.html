/ 05 FAQ

Questions,
answered.

A quick rundown of the most common questions. Still unsure of something? Send a message — responses within 24 h.

How long until I receive my figure after payment?
Production takes between 3 and 6 months depending on complexity and queue. You'll receive progress photos at each milestone so you always know where your piece is in the workshop.
Is the $3,000 price the total I'll pay?
$3,000 USD is the base price for the figure. Shipping, crating and insurance are quoted separately based on your destination — typical worldwide freight ranges from $200 to $800 depending on country.
What payment methods are accepted?
Payment is handled through PayPal's secure hosted checkout — credit card, debit card and PayPal balance are all accepted. For alternative arrangements (wire, partial payment plans) please get in touch.
Do you ship to my country?
Yes — we ship worldwide via insured freight with tracking. Once you reserve, we'll provide a customs-ready shipping quote for your specific destination.
Can I request customizations?
Minor customizations are possible — alternate pose details, display base, protective case. Send an inquiry through the contact form below before reserving and we'll confirm feasibility.
Is this an official licensed product?
No. This is an independent, fan-made artistic creation — a one-of-a-kind handmade collectible. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sold as an official product from the original rights-holders.
